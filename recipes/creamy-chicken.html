Creamy chicken with penne

This creamy chicken pasta is comfort food at its best! Loaded with tender bits of chicken and a heavenly creamy sauce made with Greek yogurt honey and freshly corn, this pasta dish is a proper treat, its healthy and is ready in just 25 minutes!

  • 250gr Pasta penne
  • Salt
  • Two chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Thyme
  • 200gr Greek yogurt
  • 70gr honey
  • Mustard
  • Corn
  • Mint
