Creamy chicken with penne

This creamy chicken pasta is comfort food at its best! Loaded with tender bits of chicken and a heavenly creamy sauce made with Greek yogurt honey and freshly corn, this pasta dish is a proper treat, its healthy and is ready in just 25 minutes!

  • 250gr Pasta penne
  • Salt
  • Two chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Thyme
  • 200gr Greek yogurt
  • 70gr honey
  • Mustard
  • Corn
  • Mint

Steps

  1. In a pot of boiling water, add salt and the penne
  2. Boil for ten minutes
  3. Place a pan on high heat
  4. Cut each fillet crosswise into four slices and spread salt, pepper, 1 tbsp. thyme and olive oil
  5. Transfer to the hot pan and cook for 3-4 minutes on both sides
  6. Put the yogurt, honey, mustard, salt, pepper in a bowl and mix with a spoon
  7. Add the corn, thyme and chopped mint
  8. Remove the chicken from the pan, cut it into strips and put it in the bowl
  9. Strain the penne, put them in the bowl and mix
  10. Serve with mint, thyme and olive oil
