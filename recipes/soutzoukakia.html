Soutzoukakia

Soutzoukakia is a Greek traditional food that has its roots in Smyrna. The origins of soutzoukakia trace back to the Greek population of the early 20th Century Empire. This is a dish that was created by a minority population and survived atrocities, thanks to the hundreds of thousands of survivors who carried the recipe with them from Turkey to Greece.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Minced beef
  • 100 gr Oats
  • Olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Cumin
  • Basil
  • Tomato juice

Steps

  1. Put the Minced beef into a big bowl
  2. Put the Oats into a blender, blend them and put them into the bowl
  3. Add the Parsley and the Basil and the star of the recipe the cumin
  4. Now you mix all the ingredients together with your hands
  5. We take a baking dish and we cover it with a very small layer of olive oil
  6. Now we make oblong meatballs and we put them in the backing dish
  7. We put the tomato juice on top of the meatballs
  8. Finally we put the baking dish in the oven for 25 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius
